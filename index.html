So… what is a Bomb Party?

A Bomb Party is a live jewelry reveal. We drop a fizzy "jewelry bomb" into warm water, it dissolves in a cloud of color and scent, and a sealed capsule floats up with a real piece of jewelry tucked inside. You don't know what you're getting until the very last second — that's the whole thrill.

Every bomb is guaranteed to hold a genuine, hand-finished piece. Some are sweet little everyday sparkle. Some are show-stoppers valued up to $1,500. You only find out when it's revealed.

How a Golden Hour reveal works

Never done this before? Here's exactly what happens from "add to cart" to the moment your jewelry lands at your door. Five steps — that's it.

  1. 01
    🛒

    Pick your bomb

    Choose your treasure type — ring, necklace, bracelet, earrings, or a bundle. You pick the style lane; the exact piece stays a mystery. Bombs start at just $19.95.

  2. 02
    📱

    Join the live drop

    Hop into a live party on TikTok with Brianna — or request a private reveal video if you can't make it. Your name gets called, and your bomb goes in.

  3. 03
    💧

    Watch it fizz

    Your bomb drops into warm water and dissolves over about two minutes in a swirl of color and scent. This is the part everyone screenshots.

  4. 04
    ✨

    The reveal

    A sealed capsule floats to the top. We unwrap it on camera and your hidden piece is revealed for the very first time — live, with you.

  5. 05
    📦

    It's yours

    Every piece is real, hand-finished jewelry valued from $25 up to $1,500. We clean it, package it, and ship it straight to your door. Sizing help included.

Good questions

Before your first drop

Is the jewelry actually real?

Yes — every bomb is guaranteed to contain a genuine, hand-finished piece of jewelry. There are no empty bombs. Values range from about $25 up to $1,500.

How much does a bomb cost?

Jewelry bombs start at $19.95. You're paying for the bomb and the reveal — the jewelry inside is frequently worth much more than what you paid.

Do I have to be live to play?

Nope. Live reveals are the most fun, but you can shop the drop any time and request a private reveal video so you still get that surprise moment.

What if my ring doesn't fit?

Brianna will help you with sizing and care. Reach out and she'll walk you through your options.

How does shipping work?

Once your piece is revealed it's cleaned, packaged and shipped straight to your door. You'll get tracking so you can watch your treasure travel.

Where do I actually buy?

Everything is purchased through Brianna's official Bomb Party shop — scan the code above or hit any "Shop the drop" button on this page.
